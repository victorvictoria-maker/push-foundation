Established in the year 2015, Praise Until Something Happens Initiative (PUSH), a Nigeria registered charity organization with CAC/IT 102606 aim at putting smile on the faces of the less priviledged through evangelism, motivation, feeding, clothing, free health care delivery, community development projects and empowerment.

MISSION & VISION

At PUSH Foundation, we are driven by a single goal - to do our part in making the world a better place for all. Touching lives is our calling, eradicating poverty in Africa is our MISSION.

Our VISION is to make the world a better place in the area of welfare, justice for all and child right advocacy. Our mission is to embark on a journey of initiatives, for a sustainable environment, to give everyone eqaul opportunity to better life.

OUR INTIATIVES

FEED AND CLOTHE THE NATION

We live is a society that is self-centered, an unfair system whereby the rich is busy getting richer while the poor are wallowing in abject poverty. Statistics shows that poverty has risen in Nigeris with more than 100 million people living on less than $1 a day. In the light of this, we inspired to close the pag between the rich and the poor.

#BACK2SCHOOL

This is a yearly initiative of PUSH Foundation.

We visit local public schools to motivate and encourage the students as well as give handouts not limited to stationaries!

VOLUNTEERS HANGOUT

Every year, the PUSH Team comprising of volunteers meet to have fun and celebrate the organization's anniversary.

It's always an amazing time to hangout and network with like minds.
